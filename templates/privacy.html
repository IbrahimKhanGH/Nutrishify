Privacy Policy

By using the Nutritishify app, you acknowledge that it utilizes the open-source Spotify Web API. In order to provide the service, your Spotify account username and data related to your top artists and tracks may be accessed. It is important to note that Nutrishify does not store or collect any of the data used during its operation. Furthermore, no information is shared with third parties. All data is exclusively utilized for the purpose of generating and displaying your personalized Nutrition Label.
