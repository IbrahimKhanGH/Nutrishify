About Spotify Nutrition

Inspired by @albumreceipts, Spotify Nutrition is a tool that presents a user's top 10 most-played tracks from the last month, last 6 months, and all time, arranged in a nutrition label-like format.

FAQ

Why isn't Spotify Nutrition working? If you encounter issues when attempting to log in with Spotify, it may be due to pop-up blockers enabled on your browser or device. Please ensure that pop-ups are allowed and try again. Enabling pop-ups in Chrome | Enabling pop-ups in Safari (iOS) What does the "amount" represent in top artists and genres? In the top artists section, the "amount" refers to the popularity of the artist, with a scale ranging from 1 (least popular) to 10 (most popular). In the top genres section, the "amount" indicates the frequency of a genre appearing within your top 50 artists. Why doesn't my Spotify Nutrition display top tracks by time period? Currently, the Spotify API only provides "Heavy Rotation" data, which encompasses the most played tracks, albums, playlists, and more from an unspecified time period. While it would be ideal to break down this information by time period, unfortunately, there is currently no method to obtain such data. My Spotify most-played doesn't seem accurate! How are these stats determined? Receiptify utilizes data directly collected by Spotify to determine the top artists for the past month, past 6 months, and all time. As the data is updated by Spotify, the exact calculation method is unknown. If you notice discrepancies in your recently played data for the month, it might be due to a slight time offset in the "past month" top track calculations. Alternatively, it's possible that you've been listening to certain tracks more frequently than you realized! Can you display the number of times a track is played in Spotify Nutrition? Unfortunately, the Spotify API does not provide this specific information. However, if you have last.fm, you can find track play counts in the last.fm version of Spotify Nutrition. My Spotify Nutrition isn't functioning! Occasionally, Spotify Nutrition may encounter errors during periods of high traffic. If you have already logged in, try refreshing the site multiple times until your nutrition label appears. If this approach doesn't resolve the issue, you can attempt clearing your cookies and site data or use a different device. Can you create a version of Spotify Nutrition for [another music streaming service]? Some music streaming services lack accessible APIs to retrieve user listening data. While I strive to support as many services as possible, please note that I am a full-time student and may not always be able to accommodate such requests, especially during busy periods. How did you develop Spotify Nutrition? Spotify Nutrition is an open-source project! You can access the source code here. Is there a risk of Spotify stealing my data? Rest assured that Spotify does not have access to your data through Spotify Nutrition. For detailed information, please review the Privacy Policy of Spotify Nutrition.
